# Program Analyst Job Posting Summary

## Qualifications
**Time-In-Grade Requirement:** Applicants who are current Federal employees must have served 52 weeks at the GS-09 level to be eligible for a GS-11 position.
- **SF-50 Required:** Submissions of an SF-50 showing time-in-grade eligibility is mandatory.
- **Selective Placement Factors (SPF):** To qualify, applicants must demonstrate proficiency in data mining techniques and tools such as Power BI, SharePoint, SQL, etc.

## Required Skills
1. Utilization of Data Mining Techniques: Including the use of business intelligence tools like Power BI, SharePoint, SQL.
2. Structured Query Language (SQL) and Reporting Techniques.
3. Knowledge of Organizational Procedures, Techniques, and Systems: For documenting processes to improve project management, productivity, efficiency, controls, and planning.
4. Preparation of Briefing Charts, Fact Sheets, and Staff Reports: Including findings, analyses, recommendations, and other necessary correspondences.
5. Review and Analysis of Data/Reports from Various Automated Systems and Sources: To track trends, effectiveness, and needed improvements to procedures and processes, aligning with VA regulations and policies.

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, produce, analyze, and distribute program and project data.
- Conduct reviews and analyses using various automated systems and sources to monitor the performance of a 24/7 crisis line service for Veterans, Service Members, and their family members.
- Utilize Power BI, SharePoint, SQL, and related tools to prepare comprehensive reports and dashboards for program analysis and upward reporting.
